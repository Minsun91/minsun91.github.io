Sri Lanka: Tea, Tuk-tuks & Chaos

March 2025 • 5 min read

Sri Lanka was chaos in the best possible way — horns blaring, tea steaming, waves crashing. Every corner smelled like cardamom and diesel, and somehow it all worked.

From the hill country trains to the southern beaches, every town felt like a new tempo. The tea fields in Nuwara Eliya were cinematic, and the tuk-tuks in Colombo? Terrifyingly fun.

“Sri Lanka doesn’t ask you to relax — it demands it.”

I left with sunburned shoulders, a phone full of strangers’ smiles, and a craving for coconut roti. There’s a certain peace in the noise, once you stop resisting it.
