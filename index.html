    My work experiences

    • 2023-Present

      Spiritory

      I worked as a backend developer at a startup in Germany, where I implemented features for automatic email sending for all flows, order confirmation PDF generation, data migration from firebase to strapi, and shipping/tracking link generation.

    • 2022

      Coding Bootcamp

      I learned Node.js through an online bootcamp and completed team projects such as Clips and Tindater. I worked with my team members to complete every step of the web development process, from planning to deployment.

    • 2019-2021

      PanAsia

      As a buyer for Asian food at a wholesale company in Europe, my main responsibilities were demand forecasting and new product development. I also automated the quantity calculation process by creating an Excel spreadsheet and introduced Notion to improve communication between team members in Austria and Korea.

    • 2017-2019

      Korea Foods

      I had the opportunity to work as a buyer for a leading Korean food distributor in the UK. My primary focus was on sourcing and importing Korean snacks and beverages to meet the ever-growing demand in the British market. Alongside this, I also acquired valuable skills in Excel automation.
